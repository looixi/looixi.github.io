Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle, and you'll have access to your current plan's features until then.
Do you offer a free trial?
We offer a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan with full access to all premium features. No credit card required to start your trial!
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise customers, we also support bank transfers and purchase orders.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and follow industry best practices. Your mood boards and data are protected with bank-level security.
Can I use Illusera for commercial projects?
Yes! All Pro and Enterprise plans include commercial usage rights. The Free plan is limited to personal use only. Please check individual stock photo licenses for specific usage terms.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not completely satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
